Batho Ba Shoa - Ruthla Masupha [song]

This music is made by Basotho for Basotho but has been appreciated on a great scale by foreigners for its distinct instrument composition.

Ndlamo

Traditional dance whereby men/boys stomp their feet and soaring high kicks

Mokhibo

This is a women dance perfomed on the knees, the body gently rising and falling as hands are swept or side ways

Mohobelo

This is men dance whereby men stomp their feet strongly on the ground and it shows joy

Mokorotlo

This is men dance which is sang and indicates unity and celebration during Basotho events

Litolobonya

a dance perfomed by married women and girls which express their feelings,especially in relationships and marriages

Khabulu-Bulu

This instrument produces a deep sound of a bullroarer

Lekhitla la Lenaka

It is made of an animal horn and produces a loud sound when blown and it may be used to call people or for signals

Lekhitlane la Lesapo

It is Flute made of an animals bone and gives out a sound which similar to a whistle

Thomo

A stringer instrument played during storytelling

Lekolulo

A flute made from wood or reed played by sherperd

lesiba

A mouth-resonated bow played by herding boys for entertainment, haunting melodies

Setolotolo

An extended jaw harp played by men using their mouth

Sekupu

Adrum made with animal skin strethed over a wooden or clayed container

Sekebeku sa Lehlaka

Made from reed which is played by blowing

Moropa

Another name for a drum but this instrument is louder than of sekupu instrument

Sekebeku sa Ts'epe

A made of metal and is held between the lips or teeth and metal strip is plucked

Mamokhorong

A stringed instument made with wood and sinew played by plucking

Seletsa sa Likhoele

A homemade string instrument made from wooden box and wire

Sekhankula

A musicla bow similar to thomo but larger,which is played by plucking or striking the string

Leqhubu

A small whistle made from reed

Thotolo

This is a kind of slit drum made from hollowed-out piece of wood,which is struck by hands or sticks to produce deep sounds

Setebe

This is an instrument made from silk moth cocoons and filled with seeds,and fasten on the legs and produce sound when shaken during ceremonies

Leshoehle-shoehle

this instrument is made of gourd and seeds,of which seeds are put inside a gourd.They are played by shaking the gourd
